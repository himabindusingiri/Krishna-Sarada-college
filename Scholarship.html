General Eligibility Criteria

While specific scholarships may have additional requirements, most scholarships at Krishna Sharada Junior & Degree College require applicants to meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a currently enrolled student or newly admitted student at Krishna Sharada Junior & Degree College
  • Must maintain satisfactory academic progress (minimum GPA requirements vary by scholarship)
  • For need-based scholarships, must provide proof of family income
  • Must not be in default on any student loan or owe a refund on any educational grant
  • Must complete all required application materials by the published deadline
  • For merit-based scholarships, must have scored in the top 10% of the qualifying examination
  • For sports/arts scholarships, must provide evidence of achievements at recognized competitions

Application Process

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for each scholarship to determine which ones you qualify for.

Gather Documents

Prepare all required documents which may include academic transcripts, income certificates, caste certificates (if applicable), achievement proofs, etc.

Complete Application Form

Fill out the scholarship application form available at the college office or download from our website.

Write Personal Statement

Some scholarships require a personal statement explaining your financial need, academic goals, or other relevant information.

Submit Before Deadline

Submit your complete application package to the Scholarship Committee before the announced deadline.

Interview (if required)

Some scholarships may require an interview with the selection committee.

Receive Decision

Scholarship recipients will be notified by email and college notice board within 4-6 weeks of application deadline.
