Our Legacy & Commitment

Our History

Established in 1995, Krishna Sharada Junior & Degree College is a distinguished private institution in Porumamilla, Andhra Pradesh. With a legacy spanning over 25 years, we have been a beacon of academic excellence, empowering rural youth with quality education and holistic development opportunities. Our commitment to fostering intellectual and personal growth has earned us a stellar reputation, reflected in our 4.4/5 rating based on 21 student reviews.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, ethical values, and leadership skills, preparing students to excel in a dynamic global landscape.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that fosters innovation, promotes research, and contributes significantly to the socio-economic development of Porumamilla and beyond.

Our Achievements

  • Consistently high pass rates in intermediate and degree examinations
  • Recognized by the Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate Education for academic excellence
  • Over 5000 alumni thriving in diverse professional fields
  • Awarded for community service and educational outreach programs
  • Modern campus with state-of-the-art facilities supporting holistic education

Why Choose Us

  • Private institution with personalized attention and small class sizes
  • Highly qualified faculty with extensive teaching experience
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering arts, science, and commerce streams
  • Vibrant campus life with diverse extracurricular activities
  • Strong industry connections for internships and placements
  • Focus on research, innovation, and community engagement

Our Values

At Krishna Sharada, we uphold values of integrity, inclusivity, and excellence. Our student-centric approach ensures a supportive environment where every individual is encouraged to reach their full potential, fostering responsible global citizens.

Our Key Features

Expert Faculty

Our team of highly qualified and experienced educators are dedicated to student success, with many holding advanced degrees and industry experience.

Modern Labs

State-of-the-art science and computer laboratories equipped with the latest technology to support practical learning and research.

Comprehensive Library

A well-stocked library with over 15,000 books, journals, and digital resources to support academic and research needs.

Career Guidance

Dedicated placement cell providing career counseling, skill development workshops, and placement opportunities with leading organizations.

Comprehensive support services including counseling, mentoring, and special assistance for differently-abled students.

Green Campus

Eco-friendly campus with lush greenery, solar power initiatives, and waste management programs promoting sustainability.
