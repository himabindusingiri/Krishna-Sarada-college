Admission Requirements

Intermediate Courses (MPC/BiPC/CEC)

  • Passed 10th standard/SSC or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Original Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Original Conduct Certificate
  • 10th standard marks memo (original + 2 photocopies)
  • Study/Bonafide Certificate (if applicable)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card copy

Degree Courses (B.Sc/B.Com)

  • Passed Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Original Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Original Conduct Certificate
  • Intermediate marks memo (original + 2 photocopies)
  • 10th standard marks memo (for verification)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Migration Certificate (if from other board)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card copy

Vocational Courses

  • Passed 10th standard/SSC or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Original Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Original Conduct Certificate
  • 10th standard marks memo (original + 2 photocopies)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate (for certain vocational courses)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card copy
