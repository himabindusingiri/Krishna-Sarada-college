About Krishna Sharada Junior & Degree College

Established in 1995, Krishna Sharada Junior & Degree College has been a beacon of quality education in Porumamilla, Andhra Pradesh. Our institution is recognized for its academic excellence and commitment to student development.

We offer a wide range of courses in arts, science, and commerce streams, catering to the diverse educational needs of students from Porumamilla and surrounding regions.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a commitment to holistic development, we strive to nurture the leaders of tomorrow through innovative teaching methods and extracurricular activities.
